Campout Schedule and Local Events

Monthy Campout Schedule

Troop 372 schedules monthly campouts throughout the “camping year”, together with Winter Camp and Summer Camp. Find links to campout signup forms on the Troop's Google Calendar or Facebook page. On the calendar, click on the campout/event to see details about signing up; in order to view additional details and links about the campout, you can also click "more details" after the campout window opens within the calendar.

In conjunction with our boy troop, Troop 370, Troop 372 hosts two Merit Badge Clinics each year, open to Scouts within and outside of Troop 372. Sign up at meritbadge.info
